About the Project

Transforming dog handler training through augmented reality technology

Project Overview

KyNova AR-Simulator is a comprehensive mobile application designed to revolutionize dog handler training through augmented reality technology. Our system provides realistic, interactive training scenarios that prepare handlers for real-world situations without the need for physical equipment or live animals during practice sessions.

The Problem

Traditional dog handler training faces several challenges: limited training opportunities, high costs for equipment and facilities, safety concerns during training, and inconsistent training quality. Our AR solution addresses these challenges by providing accessible, safe, and cost-effective training that can be performed anytime, anywhere.

Our Solution

Using Flutter and Unity AR Foundation, we've created an immersive training platform that simulates real dog training scenarios. The system tracks performance metrics, provides instant feedback, and gamifies the learning experience through achievements, XP points, and a global leaderboard.

Key Features

Comprehensive tools for effective dog handler training

AR Training Scenarios

Immersive augmented reality scenarios including obedience control, search detection, bring command, and virtual room training.

Performance Tracking

Real-time metrics for accuracy, reaction time, timing, and control with detailed analytics and progress reports.

Gamification System

XP points, achievement badges, streak bonuses, and global leaderboard to motivate continuous learning.

AI-Powered Quizzes

Automated quiz generation using AWS Bedrock AI for scenario-specific knowledge assessment.

Instructor Dashboard

Assign scenarios, monitor trainee progress, view detailed analytics, and provide personalized recommendations.

Real-Time Notifications

Multi-channel notifications via WebSocket, push notifications, and email for assignments and achievements.

Multi-Language Support

Available in 6 languages (English, Arabic, Spanish, French, Hindi, Chinese) with AI-powered translation.

Cloud Architecture

Built on AWS with Cognito authentication, DynamoDB database, Lambda functions, and S3 storage.

Training Scenarios

Five comprehensive training modules for complete handler preparation

Easy

Scenario 1: Obedience Control

800 XP

Train basic obedience commands including Come, Front, Sit, Down, Heel, Stay, and Between. 10-step progressive training with focus on accuracy and timing.

  • 10 Training Steps
  • 7 Core Commands
  • Visitor Accessible
Medium

Scenario 2: Search Detection

900 XP

Panel simulation training for scent detection. Hide items, send the dog to search, confirm correct finds, and reward proper behavior.

  • 5 Training Steps
  • Scent Detection
  • Reward System
Medium

Scenario 3: Bring Command

600 XP

Object retrieval training with ball, toy, and kong. Practice throw, retrieve, and specific object selection commands.

  • 5 Training Steps
  • 3 Object Types
  • Visitor Accessible
Hard

Scenario 4: Virtual Room

1100 XP

Advanced indoor training combining walking, stopping, seeking, and scent confirmation in a virtual room environment.

  • 5 Training Steps
  • Room Navigation
  • Scent Detection
Easy

Scenario 5: Body Language Quiz

100 XP

Learn to read dog body language through interactive quiz. Recognize behaviors like attentive sit, alert stance, and relaxed postures.

  • 5 Quiz Questions
  • Animated Behaviors
  • Guest Accessible
